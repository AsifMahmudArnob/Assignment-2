Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Kindergarten for ages 3 to 6 is a crucial stage where children explore, play, and build foundational skills. It promotes social, emotional, and cognitive development in a fun, engaging environment.

Elementary School

Elementary school is where children build core academic skills and develop a love for learning. It lays the groundwork for critical thinking, teamwork, and personal growth.

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle years, from 10 to 16, are a transformative phase where kids explore their identities and build independence. It's a time of developing critical thinking, emotional resilience, and deeper social connections.
